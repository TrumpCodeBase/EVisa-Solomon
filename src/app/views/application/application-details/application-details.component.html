Visa Classes

BUSINESS SHORT-TERM SINGLE ENTRY

Short-Term Single Entry

This visa is for individuals who intend to visit Solomon Islands for short-term business activities. Business activities which may be permitted (non-exhaustive list) include:

  • to carry out an official trade mission recognized by the Solomon Islands government
  • to sell goods and services in Solomon Islands
  • to buy Solomon Islands goods and services
  • to support a major infrastructure development project as approved by the government of Solomon Islands
  • to negotiate or discuss the set-up, expansion or wind-up of a business in Solomon Islands
  • to carry out any business with the authorized representatives of an overseas company, body or person.

    • Criteria

    To be issued this visa you must show that you are a genuine business person or are engaging in approved employment, with the intention of leaving Solomon Islands at the end of your stay.

    Eligible Passports

  • American Samoa
  • Finland
  • Luembourg

    • French Territories (as French Passport Holders) French Polynesia, Guadeloupe, New Caledonia, Reunion, Saint Barthelemy, Saint Martin (French part), Saint Pierre and Miquelon, Wallis and New Zealand Passport holding countries / territories Cook Islands, Niue, Tokelau US Territories (holders of US passports) Futuna, Guam, Northern Mariana Islands, Puerto Rico, Virgin Islands (U.S.) Dutch Territories (as Dutch passport holders). Aruba, Curacao, Sint Maarten (Dutch part), Bonaire and Sint Eustatius and Saba UNO - UN Organisation UNA - UN Agency

    Dependents

    Dependents of Business Short Term single-entry Visa Holders, who wish to accompany the visa holder to Solomon Islands, will, in most circumstances, be eligible to apply for an Easy Visitor Visa. Please refer to the Visitor Visa page for information on how to lodge an application or contact ICA to discuss your circumstances.
    This Visa is

  • valid for a single entry
  • a period of thirty (30) days from the date of arrival in Solomon Islands.

    • Where To Apply

    Applications can be lodged

  • online
  • at any PNG Diplomatic Post or Mission prior to travel
  • upon arrival for eligible passport holders at Jacksons International Airport, Port Moresby

    • You are responsible for ensuring that you have the appropriate visa or right to obtain a visa on arrival in Solomon Islands prior to commencing your travel. There may be adverse consequences if you attempt to enter Solomon Islands without authority.

    How To Apply

  • Complete the Online Application or Application for Entry Permit (Form 1)
  • Pay the Migration Service Fee (MSF) of US$190* and US$50* online lodgment fee (if applicable)
  • Provide passport and documentary evidence as follows
  • Letter from business associate in Solomon Islands indicating purpose of visit, duration of stay, location of business and proposed frequency of visits

    • *All MSF and online lodgment fees are levied in US Dollars and converted into Australian Dollars (using the published conversion rate) prior to finalization of application registration. The total fees payable will be provided by the system prior entering payment details in the e-Visa portal or you will be advised by the ICA officer when submitting your application in person.

    **You may be required to present your documentary evidence to border officials when entering Solomon Islands.

    Extension Of Stay

    No extension is allowed for this visa class

    Restricted - Restricted Employment Visa (REV)

    This visa is intended for Individuals with specialist skills and technical expertise, which is not available in Solomon Islands. A Migration Service Fee (MSF) of US$350 is charged for this Visa Class, with an additional US$50 as administrative fee for applications lodged via the e-Visa portal.

    Validity

    This visa is

  • valid for a single entry
  • a period of thirty (30) days from the date of arrival in Solomon Islands

    • Visa Conditions

  • This visa is limited to four (4) entries per calendar year
  • Travel to restricted facilities in the country without the permission of the Chief Migration Officer is prohibited

    • Where To Apply

    Applications can be lodged online. You are responsible for ensuring that you have the appropriate visa prior to commencing your travel. There may be adverse consequences if you attempt to enter Solomon Islands without authority.

    How To Apply

  • Complete the Online Application or Application for Visa (Form 1)
  • Pay the Migration Service Fee (MSF) of US$350 and US$50* online lodgment fee (if applicable)
  • Provide copy of your passports bio data page and documentary evidence such as a Letter from business associate in Solomon Islands indicating purpose of visit, duration of stay, location of business and proposed frequency of visits

    • *All MSF and online lodgment fees are levied in US Dollars and converted into Australian Dollars (using the published conversion rate) prior to finalization of application registration. The total fees payable will be provided by the system prior entering payment details in the e-Visa portal

    Documentation to Submit with Application

    Refer to the REV information on the ICA website

    Extension Of Stay

    No extension is allowed for this visa class Change of status to another visa type is not permitted

    SPECIAL EXEMPTION AID WORKER/VOLUNTEER

    This visa is for individuals who intend to visit Solomon Islands to undertake activities sponsored or facilitated by the Youth With A Mission (YWAM) organization.

    This visa expressly prohibits the holder from engaging in paid work.


    Criteria

    To be issued this visa you must show that you are a genuine visitor, sponsored by YWAM, with the intention of leaving Solomon Islands at the end of your stay.

    Validity

    This visa is

  • Valid for a period of stay up to 12 months from the day of first arrival in Solomon Islands
  • Allows the holder single entry to Solomon Islands within the 12 month period

    • Where To Apply

    Applications can be lodged

  • online
  • at any PNG Diplomatic Post or Mission prior to travel

    • You are responsible for ensuring that you have the appropriate visa or right to obtain a visa on arrival in Solomon Islands prior to commencing your travel. There may be adverse consequences if you attempt to enter Solomon Islands without authority.

    How To Apply

  • Complete the Online Application or Application for Entry Permit
  • Pay the Migration Service Fee (MSF) of US$50* and US$50* online lodgment fee (if applicable)
  • Provide passport and documentary evidence as follows
  • Letter of support from YWAM

    • *All MSF and online lodgment fees are levied in US Dollars and converted into Australian Dollars (using the published conversion rate) prior to finalization of application registration. The total fees payable will be provided by the system prior entering payment details in the e-Visa portal or you will be advised by the ICA officer when submitting your application in person.

    **You may be required to present your documentary evidence to border officials when entering Solomon Islands.

    Extension Of Stay

    In limited situations an extension of stay may be permitted. Please contact ICA to discuss your individual circumstances

    Easy Visitor Permit (30 days)

    This visa is for individual who intent to visit Solomon Islands for holidays, sightseeing, social or recreational reasons, and to visit relatives and friends. There is no Migration Service Fee (MSF) payable for the Visitor Permit, however, applications lodged via the e-Visa portal will attract a fee of US$50. This visa expressly prohibits the holder from engaging in paid work.

    Eligible Passports

  • Antigua and Barbuda
  • Grenada
  • Mauritius

    • *Passport holders may also apply for the Easy Visitor Permit (60) days.

    Validity

    This visa is

  • valid for a single entry
  • a period of thirty (30) days from the date of arrival in Solomon Islands

    • Where To Apply

    Applications can be lodged

  • online only

    • You are responsible for ensuring that you have the appropriate visa or right to obtain a visa on arrival in Solomon Islands prior to commencing your travel. There may be adverse consequences if you attempt to enter Solomon Islands without authority.

    How To Apply

  • Complete the Online Application or Application for Entry Permit
  • Pay the US$50* online lodgment fee (if applicable)
  • Provide a copy of your passports bio data page

    • *All MSF and online lodgment fees are levied in US Dollars and converted into Australian Dollars (using the published conversion rate) prior to finalization of application registration. The total fees payable will be provided by the system prior entering payment details in the e-Visa portal or you will be advised by the ICA officer when submitting your application in person.

    Extension Of Stay

    An extension of stay is not permitted
